About Tutorago

Our Mission
As the Nigerian educational systems get eroded over time, our mission is to bridge the gap to provide the needed educational values to support learners via private home lesson and tutoring.
What we do
Tutorago is an home lesson tutoring platform for children of all ages. We help learners source for exceptional tutors that can groom them effectively. Learning should not be expensive. We want to make learning more affordable. We find fully vetted, affordable, expert, exceptional private home tutor for learners within 24-72 hours or even less.
Launched in beta on 7th of January, 2017, our head office is situated at 11, Obafemi Awolowo Way, Beside Betsay Mall, Igbona, Osogbo. We don’t just do what we do but we are passionate about we do. We want to put in all our best to ensure that we add values to our clients.
We stay with our learners from the beginning till the end of their learning. Before we direct tutors their way, we interview them to ensure we are recruiting the best hand for our learners. Our interviews would be of various form such as Phone call, skype calls, physical interviews at our office. We hope to invest more into tutors training in the future – God willing.
What legacy would you love to leave behind for your children? Of course we don’t expect you to have any answer in mind rather than quality education. It is said that Education is the best legacy. We love to make learning easy, affordable and more accessible.
Trust
Your security is of utmost importance to us. One of our responsibilities towards ensuring a flawless educational platform is that we must verify and always run background checks on our tutors on behalf of our client or tutors themselves if the request is made.
When you book a tutor with us, we make sure that our tutor delivers the necessary classes and lessons to you or your child without any delay. Hence you should be rest assured that you are with the right company. Both parties are safe.
Convenience
Book for tutoring sessions for yourself or your ward(s) at any convenient time, place and everything can be managed via our platform without worry about anything. This works for both tutors and students perfectly.
Professionalism
We are always on the lookout for quality tutors for our clients nationwide. Our Tutors are thoroughly accessed via one on-one-interview, online tests or any other means possible. Leave everything to us and let our tutors deliver towards your expectation.
