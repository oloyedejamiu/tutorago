Frequently Asked Questions

How do i make money from driving traffic to tutorago?
If you refer a learners/student/ client who eventually hires a tutor from tutorago, the client would get a discount of 1,500 if they order lessons greater than or equal to 20k using your [referral code] while you would get 20% commission from what we charge our tutors after lesson completion. If you refer a tutor that get approved and delivers a lesson on our platform, you would be paid 20% of what we charge our tutors after lesson delivery. This means that you can earn 40% commission if you bring both the client/learner as well as the tutor for the tutoring.
When do i get paid for referring people to tutorago?
Any time you earn a commission from tutorago, automatically you would be alerted and the amount would be credited to your wallet on tutorago. You can make withdrawal request of the funds any time any day when it reaches 2,000 naira and above. When this is done, the money would be credited to the Nigerian bank account on your profile.
How do I start inviting people?
When you invite people(friends and student) both online and offline, try and let them know the benefit they would gain for hiring a tutor from tutorago. Inform them they they would get a discount of 1,500 if they use your referral code while ordering. You are allowed to invite these people online using your email contacts, facebook, twitter and LinkedIn - your link will be sent to them.
How can i track people i referred to tutorago?
Of course you can track your referrals. Even though you would be alerted when you have a referral, you can also track everything as regard the referral system right from the dashboard.
